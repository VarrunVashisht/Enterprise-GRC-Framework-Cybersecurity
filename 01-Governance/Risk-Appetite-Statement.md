# Risk Appetite Statement

## 1. Purpose
This statement defines the level of risk the organisation is willing to accept
in pursuit of its business objectives.

## 2. Risk Appetite Overview
- The organisation has a LOW appetite for risks impacting customer data
- The organisation has a LOW appetite for regulatory non-compliance
- The organisation has a MEDIUM appetite for operational and technology risks
- The organisation has a HIGH appetite for innovation-related risks

## 3. Cyber Security Risk Appetite
- Data breaches: Not acceptable
- Loss of customer trust: Not acceptable
- Short-term system outages: Tolerable within defined limits

## 4. Review
This statement is reviewed annually by the Board.
