# Information Security Policy

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability (C.I.A) of organisational information assets.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees
- Contractors and third parties
- All information systems, data, and cloud services

## 3. Information Security Principles
- Information must be protected against unauthorised access
- Information must be accurate and reliable
- Information must be available when required for business operations

## 4. Access Control
- Access to systems is granted based on business need
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is required for critical systems
- User access must be reviewed periodically

## 5. Data Protection
- Sensitive and personal data must be encrypted
- Data must be classified according to sensitivity
- Data breaches must be reported immediately

## 6. Incident Management
- Security incidents must be reported to the IT or Security team
- Incidents will be investigated and remediated promptly

## 7. Compliance
Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action.

## 8. Review
This policy will be reviewed annually or following a major security incident.
