# Governance Charter

## 1. Purpose
This Governance Charter defines the governance structure, decision-making authority,
and accountability for Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) activities within the organisation.

## 2. Organisation Context
The organisation is a medium-sized Australian company providing digital services,
using cloud infrastructure, and processing customer personal data.

## 3. Governance Structure
- The Board provides overall oversight of governance and risk.
- Executive Management is responsible for implementing governance decisions.
- Business Units are responsible for managing risks within their operations.

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities
- Board of Directors: Strategic oversight and risk appetite approval
- CEO: Overall accountability for governance
- CIO / IT Manager: Ownership of cyber and IT risks
- Compliance Manager: Regulatory and policy compliance
- Employees: Adherence to policies and procedures

## 5. Review and Maintenance
This charter will be reviewed annually or upon significant organisational change.
