# Risk Management Framework

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this framework is to define a structured approach
for identifying, assessing, managing, monitoring, and reporting risks
across the organisation.

## 2. Scope
This framework applies to all 
- business units, 
- systems, processes,
- and third-party relationships.

## 3. Risk Management Lifecycle
The organisation follows a continuous risk management lifecycle:

1. Identify risks
2. Assess risks
3. Treat risks
4. Monitor risks
5. Report risks

## 4. Risk Categories
- Cyber and Information Security Risk
- Operational Risk
- Compliance and Regulatory Risk
- Third-Party Risk
- Reputational Risk

## 5. Roles and Responsibilities
- Board: Oversight of enterprise risks
- Executive Management: Risk treatment decisions
- Risk Owner: Day-to-day risk management
- Employees: Risk awareness and reporting

## 6. Review
This framework is reviewed annually or following a significant risk event.
