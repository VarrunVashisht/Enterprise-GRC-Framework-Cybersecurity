# Risk Assessment Methodology

## 1. Purpose
This methodology defines how risks are assessed consistently
across the organisation.

## 2. Risk Rating Formula
Risk is calculated using the following formula:

Risk Level = Likelihood × Impact

## 3. Likelihood Scale

| Rating | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Rare |
| 2 | Unlikely |
| 3 | Possible |
| 4 | Likely |
| 5 | Almost Certain |

## 4. Impact Scale

| Rating | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Insignificant |
| 2 | Minor |
| 3 | Moderate |
| 4 | Major |
| 5 | Severe |

## 5. Risk Level Interpretation

| Score | Risk Level |
|------|-----------|
| 1–5 | Low |
| 6–10 | Medium |
| 11–15 | High |
| 16–25 | Critical |

## 6. Risk Treatment Options
- Avoid
- Reduce
- Transfer
- Accept

## 7. Review
Risk assessments are reviewed at least annually or after major changes.
