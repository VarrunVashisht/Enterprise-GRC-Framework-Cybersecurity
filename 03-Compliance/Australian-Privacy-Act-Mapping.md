# Australian Privacy Act Mapping

## Purpose
This document maps key Australian Privacy Act obligations
to organisational controls and processes.

## Privacy Act Mapping

| Privacy Principle | Requirement | Control | Evidence |
|------------------|-------------|---------|----------|
| APP 1 | Open and transparent management | Privacy Policy | Published policy |
| APP 6 | Use or disclosure of personal info | Access controls | Access logs |
| APP 11 | Security of personal information | Encryption, MFA | System configs |
| NDB Scheme | Breach notification | Incident Response Plan | IR records |

## Review
This mapping is reviewed annually or when regulatory changes occur.
