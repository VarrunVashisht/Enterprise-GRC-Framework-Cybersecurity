## 2. Scope
This framework applies to all regulatory, contractual, and internal policy requirements.

## 3. Compliance Lifecycle
The organisation follows a repeatable compliance process:

1. Identify applicable regulations and standards
2. Map requirements to internal controls
3. Implement and operate controls
4. Collect compliance evidence
5. Monitor compliance status
6. Report compliance issues
7. Review and improve

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities
- Board: Oversight of regulatory compliance
- Compliance Manager: Compliance coordination and reporting
- Control Owners: Implementation of controls
- Employees: Adherence to policies

## 5. Review
This framework is reviewed annually or when regulatory changes occur.
