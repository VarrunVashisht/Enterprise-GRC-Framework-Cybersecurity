## Overview
This project demonstrates a complete, end-to-end implementation of a
Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) framework for a medium-sized
Australian organisation operating cloud-based systems and processing
customer personal data.

The project reflects real-world GRC practices used by cyber security,
risk, and compliance teams.

---

## Scope
The GRC framework covers:
- Governance and policy management
- Enterprise and cyber risk management
- Regulatory and standards compliance
- Security controls and control testing
- Monitoring, reporting, and dashboards
- Incident management and breach notification
- Internal audit and assurance
- Continuous improvement and maturity planning

---

## Regulations and Standards
- Australian Privacy Act (APPs & NDB Scheme)
- ISO/IEC 27001 (Annex A controls)
- Internal governance and security policies
