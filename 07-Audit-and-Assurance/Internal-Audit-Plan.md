# Internal Audit Plan

## Purpose
This plan defines the organisationâ€™s approach to
conducting internal audits of governance, risk,
and compliance activities.

## Audit Scope
Internal audits may cover:
- Governance and policy compliance
- Risk management processes
- Security and IT controls
- Regulatory compliance

## Audit Approach
- Risk-based audit planning
- Review of documentation and evidence
- Interviews with control owners
- Reporting of audit findings

## Audit Frequency
- Internal audits are conducted annually
- Additional audits may be performed following incidents

## Reporting
Audit results are reported to management
and tracked until issues are resolved.
