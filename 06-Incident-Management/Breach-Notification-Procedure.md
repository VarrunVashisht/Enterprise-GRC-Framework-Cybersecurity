# Breach Notification Procedure

## Purpose
This procedure defines how eligible data breaches
are identified and reported.

## Notification Requirements
- Assess whether the breach is eligible
- Notify affected individuals where required
- Notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

## Timeframes
Notifications are made as soon as practicable
after becoming aware of an eligible data breach.

## Review
This procedure is reviewed annually.
